ClickUp is an "all-in-one" productivity platform designed to centralize tasks, documents, goals, and communication. Its flexibility stems from a unique Hierarchy that allows teams to scale from simple to-do lists to complex enterprise workflows. 
Here are 50 of ClickUp's top features, categorized by their primary function:
Task Management & Hierarchy
Hierarchy System: Organize work into Workspaces, Spaces, Folders, Lists, and Tasks for ultimate scalability.
Custom Statuses: Tailor task stages (e.g., "In Review," "Blocked") to fit your specific team's workflow.
Subtasks & Nested Subtasks: Break down complex tasks into smaller, manageable layers of work.
Checklists: Add simple to-do lists within tasks for micro-steps that donâ€™t require full subtasks.
Task Dependencies: Link tasks that rely on each other (e.g., "blocking" or "waiting on").
Recurring Tasks: Automate tasks that happen on a schedule (daily, weekly, etc.).
Task Templates: Quickly recreate standardized task structures for common projects.
Multiple Assignees: Assign one task to multiple team members for collaboration.
Task Reminders: Set personal or team alerts for upcoming deadlines.
Bulk Action Toolbar: Edit, move, or delete dozens of tasks at once with a single click. 
Views & Visualization
List View: The standard vertical view for organizing and sorting tasks.
Board View: A Kanban-style interface for dragging tasks through status columns.
Calendar View: Visualize deadlines and schedule tasks on a traditional calendar.
Gantt Chart: Manage project timelines and visualize task dependencies over time.
Timeline View: A linear view of your schedule, perfect for high-level roadmap planning.
Table View: A spreadsheet-like interface for managing large amounts of data quickly.
Workload View: Monitor team capacity to ensure no one is over or under-worked.
Mind Maps: Brainstorm and visualize workflows that sync directly with your tasks.
Map View: See location-based tasks plotted on an interactive map.
Box View: Get a bird's-eye view of what everyone is working on. 
Collaboration & Communication
ClickUp Docs: Create internal wikis, SOPs, and collaborative documents.
Whiteboards: A visual canvas for real-time brainstorming and flowcharts.
ClickUp Chat: Native real-time messaging within the platform to replace Slack.
In-Task Comments: Discuss work directly where it happens with threaded replies.
@Mentions: Notify specific team members in tasks, docs, or chat.
Email in ClickUp: Send and receive emails directly from a task.
Screen Recording (Clip): Record and share videos of your screen without leaving the app.
Proofing: Add comments and annotations directly to images and PDF attachments.
Assigned Comments: Turn a comment into a required action item for someone else.
Public Sharing: Share views, docs, or tasks with external stakeholders via a link.

Custom Automations: Create "If-Then" triggers to automate repetitive work.
AI Writing Assistant: Generate content, draft emails, and improve writing.
AI Task Summaries: Get instant recaps of long task descriptions or comment threads.
AI Knowledge Manager: Ask questions about your entire workspace and get instant answers.
Formula Fields: Perform complex calculations using task data.

Universal Search: Search across tasks, docs, and even connected apps like Google Drive.
AI Standups: Automatically generate daily updates of what you've accomplished. 
Reporting & Advanced Utilities

Goals: Track high-level objectives with measurable targets and key results.
